About StudentHub

Free tools for Karnataka engineering aspirants 🎓

StudentHub is a student-built toolkit designed to help KCET aspirants make smarter, data-backed decisions during the KEA counseling process. No paywalls, no fake promises — just honest tools.

What we offer

📊 KCET College Predictor

Enter your expected KCET rank and branch to get a personalized list of colleges classified as Safe, Moderate, or Dream — based on 2024 and 2025 Round 3 closing ranks.

📋 Interactive Cutoff Explorer

Browse and filter KCET cutoff trends across 40+ colleges by branch and category (GM, 2A, SC). Designed to help you shortlist colleges quickly.

🎓 SGPA Calculator

Calculate your semester GPA using the standard 10-point grading scale followed by VTU, REVA, and most autonomous colleges in Karnataka.

Data Sources

All cutoff data is derived from the official KEA (Karnataka Examinations Authority) Seat Matrix published after Round 3 counseling for 2024 and 2025. You can verify data at the official KEA portal: kea.kar.nic.in →

⚠️ Disclaimer: All predictions and cutoff figures on this site are for reference and guidance only. Actual KCET 2026 cutoffs will depend on the final seat matrix, paper difficulty, and student demand. Always verify with the official KEA website before making decisions.
