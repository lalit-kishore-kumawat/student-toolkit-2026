About StudentHub

Built by students, for students

Navigating KCET counseling shouldn't be stressful or locked behind expensive paywalls. StudentHub is a free toolkit designed to help Karnataka engineering aspirants make smarter, data-backed decisions with complete transparency.

What we offer

🎯

KCET College Predictor

Enter your expected rank and branch to instantly discover colleges classified as Safe, Moderate, or Dream — calculated from rigorous KEA closing rank data.

📊

Interactive Cutoff Explorer

Effortlessly browse and filter historical KCET cutoffs across 40+ top engineering colleges by branch and category. The easiest way to shortlist your options.

📝

Marks to Rank Calculator

Estimate your KCET engineering rank using a composite score of your KCET and qualifying exam PCM marks.

Reliable & Transparent Data

Trust is our priority. All our cutoff algorithms and predictions are strictly powered by official KEA (Karnataka Examinations Authority) closing ranks from 2024 & 2025.

Verify our data directly at the official portal:
kea.kar.nic.in ↗
