Expected KCET Cutoff 2025: Engineering Colleges in Karnataka (Closing Ranks)

🗓️ Last updated: March 2026

Finding the right engineering college in Karnataka depends entirely on understanding historical KCET Cutoff trends. Because actual cutoffs are only released during KEA option entry, students must rely on previous year closing ranks to build a safe preference list.

On this page, we have analyzed the official Round 3 seat matrix data from recent years to provide expected closing ranks for highly competitive branches like Computer Science (CSE), Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML), and Information Science (ISE).

Top Engineering Colleges in Bangalore through KCET

Many students prefer colleges located in Bangalore due to better placement opportunities, IT hub proximity, and industry exposure. Top KCET colleges in Bangalore include RVCE, PES University, BMSCE, MSRIT, and DSCE. The cutoffs for these city-center institutions are typically the most competitive in the state.

KCET Cutoff 2025 for Different Rank Ranges

Students often search for engineering colleges based on their specific rank brackets. Here is a quick overview of what you can expect:

  • Below 5,000 rank: Elite Tier-1 colleges like RVCE, PES University, and BMSCE.
  • 5,000 – 10,000 rank: Top colleges like DSCE, UVCE, and Ramaiah Institute of Technology (MSRIT).
  • 10,000 – 20,000 rank: Excellent mid-tier options like SIT Tumkur, NMIT, and Bangalore Institute of Technology (BIT).
  • Above 20,000 rank: Reputable colleges like REVA University, CMRIT, and New Horizon College.

KCET Cutoff 2025 FAQs

Is this KCET cutoff accurate?
These are expected cutoffs based on previous year trends and may vary slightly based on seat matrix adjustments and paper difficulty.
Which college is best under 10,000 rank in Bangalore?
Colleges like PES University, BMSCE, UVCE, and MSRIT offer excellent options for tech branches in this rank range.
Can I get CSE with a 15,000 rank?
Yes, a 15,000 rank is highly competitive for CSE in mid-tier Karnataka colleges like DSCE, NMIT, and SIT Tumkur.
