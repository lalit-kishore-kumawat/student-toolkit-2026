Why These Cutoffs Matter for KCET 2026

KCET cutoffs are the last rank at which a seat was allotted in Round 2/3 of 2025 counselling. They are the best predictor for 2026. Always add a 10–15% safety buffer because cutoffs shift slightly every year due to seat matrix changes and student preferences.

KCET 2025 Cutoff by Rank Range (GM Category)

Practical guide based on official 2025 Round 2/3 data:

  • Below 1,000 rank: RVCE (CSE/AIML/DS/ECE), PES University CSE
  • 1,000 – 3,000 rank: PESU AIML/ECE, MSRIT CSE/AIML/ISE/ECE, BMSCE CSE
  • 3,000 – 7,000 rank: BMSCE DS/ISE/AIML/ECE, UVCE CSE, JSS S&T CSE/ECE, DSCE CSE/AIML
  • 7,000 – 12,000 rank: DSCE ISE/ECE, BIT CSE/AIML/DS, SIT Tumkur AIML/CSE
  • 12,000 – 22,000 rank: BIT ISE/ECE, SIT ISE/ECE, Dr. Ambedkar CSE, KLS Gogte AIML/CSE
  • 22,000+ rank: SDM Dharwad, Basaveshwara, Malnad College of Engineering
